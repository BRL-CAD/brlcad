RCC (Right Circular Cylinder)

Figure (ex.rcc): Example Right Circular Cylinder

mged> in rcyl rcc
Enter X, Y, Z of vertex: 0 0 0
Enter X, Y, Z of height (H) vector: 0 0 60
Enter radius: 15
This sequence produces the RCC shown in Figure (ex.rcc) . Note that in this case, the A,B,C, and D vectors have magnitude which equal the radius, 15.
