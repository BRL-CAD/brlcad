Scale Operation

Figure (esc-scale): Cylinder Scale Increased by 1.5X

mged> Select the ``Scale'' entry in the solid edit menu
mged> p 1.5
mged>

The point V remains fixed, the distance H between the two end-plate ellipses is multiplied by 1.5. See Figure (esc-scale) .

The command

mged> p 1
mged>

restores the original scale.
