Produce UNIX Plot

plot [-zclip] [-2d] [out-file] [| filter]

This command is used to produce a UNIX-plot hardcopy of the present view of the 'geometry' on the display device. The MGED faceplate will not be drawn. Some useful examples are:

plot-fb LIBFB framebuffer (low res)
plot-fb -h LIBFB framebuffer (high res)
tplot -Ti10 Imagen laser printer
tplot -Tmeg Megatek 7250
tplot -T4014 Tek4014
tplot -Thpgl HP7550A plotter
