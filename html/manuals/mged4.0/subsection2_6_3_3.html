Silicon Graphics Knobs

Figure (fig-sgi-knobs): Silicon Graphics Knob Assignments

Figure (fig-sgi-knobs) depicts the functions assigned to the eight knobs on the Silicon Graphics knob box. In normal operation, the left knobs provide rotations, and the right knobs provide translations and zooming. When the angle/distance cursor is activated, some of the knobs are redefined.
